Hi, my name is Madeleine and I'm an aspiring software engineer from Somerset, England.

Why does your website look like this?

Upon researching personal websites, a lot of the ones that I came across looked the same: all using the latest 'optimal' tech-stack; having a light/dark mode toggle; each spouting their childhood passion for software engineering.

That's why I wanted my portfolio to look like this - a bit different. Because I am a bit different.

I didn't fall in love with programming as a child nor was Computer Science my favourite subject since school. I don't have a million projects on my GitHub and I didn't create a graphics engine at the age of 15.

What I do have, however, is a different perspective to others. I studied both STEM and humanities subjects until I had to choose one or the other. I have a passion for problem solving in innovative ways and using my different experiences to bring alternative ideas to situations.

I have a thirst for knowledge and love learning new things - particularly in a hands-on way. If I am faced with a task, I may not understand it straight away but I will work hard until I do.

I hope you enjoy looking through my portfolio.
